About Us

We are a team of health and technology enthusiasts dedicated to creating a simple and effective way for people to assess their obesity risk based on lifestyle and physical indicators. Our system uses machine learning algorithms trained on real health datasets to predict obesity levels accurately.

This website was developed as part of a healthcare initiative to spread awareness about obesity and help users make better health decisions. We believe that accessible technology can empower people to live healthier lives.

Technologies Used: Python, Scikit-learn, Pandas, HTML, CSS, and Streamlit/Flask.
